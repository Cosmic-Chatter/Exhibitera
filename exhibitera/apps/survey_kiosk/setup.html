Welcome to Survey Kiosk!

Survey Kiosk helps you collect feedback from visitors by asking one or more questions. Responses are time-stamped and stored in a downloadable spreadsheet for you to review.

To create a Survey Kiosk, you will need:

  • A list of languages you want to support;
  • A list of questions and their available answers.
Definition name must not be blank.

What languages will you include?

Survey Kiosk enables you to present the survey in multiple langages. Select the langauges you want to include. The first language you select will be the default one (you can change this later).

You must add at least one language.

What questions will you ask?

Each question will be presented on its own screen. Add questions below in your default language (you'll add translations later).

You must add at least one question.
Questions cannot be blank.

What answer options do you want?

Next, you need to add the answer options you want to provide for each question in the default language. You'll be able to add images and configure the appearance of answers after guided setup is complete.
