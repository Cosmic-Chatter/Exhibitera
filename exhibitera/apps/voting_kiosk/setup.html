Welcome to Voting Kiosk!

Voting Kiosk displays single-question surveys that enable you to collect feedback from visitors. Responses are time-stamped and stored in a downloadable spreadsheet for you to review.

To create a Voting Kiosk, you will need:

  • The text of the question you want to ask;
  • A list of answer options.
Definition name must not be blank.

What question will you ask?

Voting Kiosk enables you to gather responses to a single question. You can place your question above (the header) or below (the footer) the answer buttons.

Some people use the header for one language and the footer for another. Others choose to omit both the header and the footer if the answer options are self explanatory.

What answer options do you want?

Survey respondants will be able to select one answer from the list you provide. You can choose from pre-configured options or create your own.

You can add images to your answer options after completing the setup wizard.
You must add at least one answer option.
Answer options must not be blank (you can remove text after completing the setup wizard).
